Atomic Design Ecosystem

Atomic Design is a design system created by Brad Frost and inspired by chemistry. It is a modular system founded on the principle that a whole system can be subdivided into smaller parts (modules) that can be independently created, replaced, modified, or exchanged with other smaller parts or across different systems.

Card Architecture

Surfaces & Transparency

Cards are the primary container for content. We utilize two distinct styles: a solid opaque surface for standard information, and a glass-morphic translucent surface for floating or high-impact elements over gradients. Cards will be on a cream background of STONE-50

01. Standard Surface

Standard Card

Used for the majority of UI elements. Solid white background with a subtle Stone-100 border and light shadow.

bg-white | border-stone-100

02. Glass Surface

Glass Card

Used when layering over gradients or images. Features backdrop blur and a semi-transparent white wash.

Day Time (06:00 - 18:00)

During daylight hours, the primary interaction color shifts to Orange-500 to match the energetic tone of the day.

Night Time (18:00 - 06:00)

As evening approaches, the system adapts. Primary actions transition to Purple-500 , reducing eye strain and shifting mood.
